Common Online Scams

Cyber criminals use different techniques to trick people online. Understanding these scams can help you stay safe.

Most Common Scam Types

  1. Phishing Emails
  2. Fake Bank Messages
  3. Investment Scams
  4. Romance Scams
  5. Online Shopping Fraud

Warning Signs of a Scam

  • Urgent messages asking for money
  • Requests for passwords or bank details
  • Emails from unknown senders
  • Links that look suspicious
