Understanding Online Scams and Cyber Deception

Stay Informed. Stay Protected.

This is a college project completed as part of an assignment.

The internet has created new opportunities for cyber criminals to deceive people. Online scams are increasing every year. Attackers use fear, urgency, and trust to trick individuals into giving away personal information or money.

According to research, phishing and social engineering remain among the most common cyber threats today.

Why Online Scams Are Common

  • Use of social media and messaging apps
  • Growth of artificial intelligence tools
  • Large amounts of personal data shared online
