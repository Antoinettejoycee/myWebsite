How Scammers Trick You

Scammers exploit fear, urgency, and trust. Learning their tactics is the first defense.

Common Tricks

  • Urgency & Fear: Messages like “Your account will be locked!”
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be banks, delivery services, or friends
  • Fake Links & Attachments: May steal credentials or install malware
  • Too Good to Be True Offers: Free prizes or giveaways
  • Social Engineering: Manipulating emotions to gain info
